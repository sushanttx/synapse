# Content Marketing Plan 2024

## Overview

This document outlines our content strategy and calendar for 2024, covering all marketing channels and initiatives.

## Content Pillars

1. **Strategy**: Thought leadership and industry insights
2. **Content**: Educational and engaging content
3. **Reports**: Data-driven analysis and case studies
4. **Briefs**: Campaign briefs and project documentation

## Monthly Content Calendar

### January - March (Q1)
- 12 blog posts (weekly)
- 3 whitepapers
- 1 quarterly report
- Daily social media posts

### April - June (Q2)
- 12 blog posts
- Project X launch content
- 3 case studies
- 1 quarterly report

### July - September (Q3)
- 12 blog posts
- Campaign performance content
- 3 infographics
- Q3 campaign report

### October - December (Q4)
- 12 blog posts
- Holiday campaign content
- Year-end review
- 2025 strategy preview

## Project-Specific Content

### Project X
- Launch announcement
- Feature highlights
- Customer success stories
- Technical documentation

### Project Y
- Introduction series
- Use case examples
- Integration guides
- FAQ content

## Content Types

1. **Blog Posts**: 800-1500 words, SEO optimized
2. **Whitepapers**: 2000-5000 words, in-depth analysis
3. **Reports**: Data visualization and insights
4. **Briefs**: Campaign planning documents

## Distribution Channels

- Company website/blog
- Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook)
- Email newsletters
- Industry publications
- Partner channels

## Success Metrics

- Website traffic growth
- Lead generation from content
- Social media engagement
- Email open and click rates
- Content downloads

## Internal Content

- Team meeting notes
- Strategy documents
- Project briefs
- Performance reports
